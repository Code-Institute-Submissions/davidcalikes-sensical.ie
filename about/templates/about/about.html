About Us

Sensical Multi-Sensory Solutions, is a Sensory equipment provision service operating in the North East of Ireland.

Sensical, can create a pop-up style sensory room in virtually any small to medium sized space in a matter of minutes.

Our Irish-owned, family-run, business has been in operation since 2018. As the company continues to grow, 2023 will see further expansion with the introduction of an online webstore.
